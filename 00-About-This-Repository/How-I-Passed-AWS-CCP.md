## Biggest Lessons Learned

1. AWS repeats concepts frequently.
2. Security services are heavily tested.
3. Service comparison questions are common.
4. Understanding use cases is more important than memorizing definitions.
5. Last-minute revision sheets are extremely valuable.

---

## Exam Strategy

### Before the Exam

* Revise service comparisons.
* Review security services.
* Read final revision sheets.

### During the Exam

* Eliminate obviously wrong answers first.
* Look for AWS keywords.
* Focus on use cases.
* Manage time carefully.

---

## Final Advice

Do not try to memorize everything.

Focus on:

* Core AWS services
* Common exam traps
* Service comparisons
* Security concepts

Understanding when to use a service is more important than memorizing every feature.

Good luck with your AWS journey.
