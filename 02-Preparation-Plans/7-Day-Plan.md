# AWS CLF-C02 — 7 Day Preparation Plan

This plan is for students who have approximately 1 week before the exam.

Seven days is enough time to build proper AWS fundamentals, understand service use cases, practice mock tests, and revise efficiently without rushing.

The goal is simple:

**Learn → Understand → Compare → Practice → Fix Gaps → Pass**

---

Start with understanding how AWS works before learning individual services.

## AWS Global Infrastructure

Study:

* Regions
* Availability Zones
* Edge Locations
* Local Zones

Understand:

### Region

A geographic area containing multiple Availability Zones.

### Availability Zone

One or more data centers inside a Region.

### Edge Location

Used by CloudFront to deliver content closer to users.

## Review Every Mistake

For every incorrect answer:

### Step 1

Identify the concept being tested.

### Step 2

Identify the AWS service involved.

### Step 3

Understand why the correct answer is correct.

### Step 4

Understand why the wrong answers are wrong.

### Step 5

Write down the takeaway.

# Important Reminder

Do not memorize answers.

Understand concepts.

Do not memorize dumps.

Understand why the answer is correct.

Use mock tests to identify weaknesses.

Use the repository to fix those weaknesses.

The AWS Cloud Practitioner exam rewards concept recognition, service understanding, and elimination skills far more than memorization.
