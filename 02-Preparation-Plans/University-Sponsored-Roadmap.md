# Phase 1 — NSDC Registration

Before registering:

* Complete any university requirements.
* Obtain faculty approval.
* Use Aadhaar-linked mobile number.
* Use official university email ID.

Visit:

https://www.skillindiadigital.gov.in

Register as:

Learner / Participant

Complete:

* OTP Verification
* Aadhaar eKYC
* Profile Setup

Search:

Cloud Practitioner Certification

Select:

The Batch ID provided by your faculty.

Important:

Selecting the wrong batch may result in loss of eligibility for certification benefits.

After submission:

* Click Show Interest
* Inform faculty
* Wait for approval
* Login again
* Applications → Approved → Accept

Registration is complete only after clicking Accept.
