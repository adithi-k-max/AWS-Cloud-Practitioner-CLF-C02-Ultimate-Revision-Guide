# AWS CLF-C02 — 3 Day Preparation Plan

This plan is for students who have approximately 3 days before the exam.

You have enough time to understand concepts properly instead of relying entirely on memorization.

The goal is simple:

**Learn → Revise → Test → Find Gaps → Fix Gaps → Pass**

---

## How To Review Mock Tests

Do not simply check the score.

For every incorrect question:

### Step 1

Identify the concept being tested.

### Step 2

Identify the AWS service involved.

### Step 3

Understand why the correct answer is correct.

### Step 4

Understand why the wrong answers are wrong.

### Step 5

Write down the key takeaway.

This is exactly how most of the learning happens.

One incorrect question can teach you four or five AWS services at once.
